Our Services

Electrical Resistivity Tomography (ERT)

Advanced subsurface imaging using electrical resistivity measurements for geological and hydrogeological investigations.

Multichannel Analysis of Surface Waves (MASW)

Geophysical method for evaluating the shear-wave velocity profile of the subsurface for geotechnical engineering applications.

Civil Works

Full range of civil engineering services from planning and design to construction supervision and quality control.

About Osis International

Osis International is a premier consulting company specializing in geophysical investigations and civil engineering works. Founded by Dr. Bikash Phuyal, the company brings together extensive academic knowledge and practical field experience to deliver exceptional results for clients worldwide.

Our team of experts utilizes state-of-the-art equipment and methodologies to provide accurate data interpretation and actionable insights for a wide range of projects including infrastructure development, environmental assessments, and natural hazard mitigation.

With a commitment to excellence and innovation, Osis International has successfully completed numerous national and international projects, earning a reputation for reliability, precision, and professional integrity.
